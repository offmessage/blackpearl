The Motor¶

How it works¶

The motor is the simplest of the hardware outputs. It takes a single input of between -63 and +63, and sets the speed of the motor accordingly (with -63 being maximum speed in one direction, and +63 being maximum speed in the other).

Supported methods¶

The module supports the following operations:

.stop()¶

Stops the motor.

.set_speed(speed)¶

Sets the speed of the motor. speed must be in the range -63 to +63. builtins.ValueError is raised if speed is outside that range.
